Understanding Canine Epilepsy

What Is Epilepsy in Dogs?

Epilepsy in dogs is a neurological condition marked by the occurrence of recurring, spontaneous seizures. These seizures can vary in their strength and duration, profoundly affecting a dog's well-being and overall quality of life.

Impact of Epilepsy on Dogs

Dogs with epilepsy may experience a range of negative effects, including physical harm during seizures, reduced life expectancy, and impaired daily functioning.
